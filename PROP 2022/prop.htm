About PROP

The Students will have the oppurtunity to be a part of this distinguished fraternity house.The mission of prop is to facilitate learning and carrier enhancement, research, knowledge, sharing, and exploring of ideas in all categories of CSE and ISE, at the same time inspiring and aspirate UG students into industry and challengers of real world. The aim of PROP is to involve young students and provide them an oppurtunity to demonstrate their projects with a strong social relevance and identify students who can develop solutions for real time problems and scenarios with a focuson social benefits.
